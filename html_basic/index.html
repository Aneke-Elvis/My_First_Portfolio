Forex Fundamentals

Forex Trading is the exchange of one to another, when ever you travel and exchange your currency to local currency you are engaging in forex trading.

Trading Approach

There are essentially three trading approaches, the Fundamental Analysis, Technical Analysis and Sentimental Analysis.

Fundamental Analysis

Fundamental Analysis is base on macro economic news and events, like GDP,employment data, war etc

Technical Analysis

Technical Analysis has to done with price action on a chart. Technical analysis traders use price action to patterns to identify opportunities in the market.
